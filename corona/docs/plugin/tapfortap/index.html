Overview

Tap for Tap is a cross-promotion and monetization network for iOS and Android apps. Drive installs by swapping clicks on our free tap exchange or by purchasing CPI, CPC or CPM traffic. Make money with our monetization platform by showing ads. Use the Earning Preference slider to determine what kind of ads you want to show and how you want to be paid. Choose "Get Users" to display Tap Exchange ads and be paid in Tap Credits; Choose "Make Money" to display ads on a revenue share basis and watch your app business grow! Visit tapfortap.com for more information.

Project Settings

SDK

When you build using the Corona Simulator, the server automatically takes care of downloading the plugin for your project.

All you need to do is add an entry into a plugins table of your build.settings. The following is an example of a minimal build.settings file:

settings =
{
    plugins =
    {
        -- key is the name passed to Lua's 'require()'
        ["plugin.tapfortap"] =
        {
            -- required
            publisherId = "com.tapfortap",
        },
    },
}
